Glossary

Author: 万叶 Date: June 29, 2022  16:48:56 Category: Language

DINK

丁克家庭。DINK = Double Income No Kids。

an affluent family They have a lot of money.
a close family They have strong relationships.
a dysfunctional family They have a lot of personal problems.
a single-parent family Only one parent lives with the children.
a talented family They’re all good at something.
an average family There is nothing special about them.
an influential family They have a certain amount of power.
an ambitious family They want to be successful

Sci-fi

科幻片。Science Fiction。
